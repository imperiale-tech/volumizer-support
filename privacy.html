Privacy Policy

Effective: May 26, 2026

Overview

Volumizer is a macOS application for per-app audio volume control. Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data Volumizer collects — which is none.

Data Collection

Volumizer does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data, usage data, analytics, or telemetry. All audio processing happens locally on your device. No information ever leaves your device.

Screen & System Audio Recording Permission

Volumizer requires the macOS "Screen & System Audio Recording" permission. Despite the name, Volumizer does not perform any screen recording or screen capture. This system permission is required by macOS to access Apple's Core Audio process tap API (CATapDescription), which Volumizer uses exclusively for audio stream interception.

How Volumizer uses this permission

Volumizer uses Core Audio process taps to intercept per-application audio streams in real time. This enables per-app volume control, equalization, and audio output routing. Audio data is processed in real-time memory buffers and immediately output to your selected audio device.

What data is accessed

Only real-time audio buffers from running applications. No visual or screen content is accessed, captured, or processed at any time.

Data storage and retention

Audio data is never recorded, stored, or retained. It exists only momentarily in memory during real-time processing. User preferences (volume levels, EQ settings, output device selections) are stored locally on your device in UserDefaults and are never transmitted.

Data sharing

No audio data, screen data, or any other user data is transmitted off your device or shared with any third party. All processing is performed entirely on-device.

Subscriptions

Volumizer requires a subscription ($0.99/month) to use, with a 3-day free trial. Subscriptions are managed entirely through Apple's App Store. Payment processing is handled by Apple. We do not have access to your payment information.

Third-Party Services

Volumizer does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking services.

Changes
