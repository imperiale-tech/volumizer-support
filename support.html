Volumizer Support

Per-app volume control for macOS

Getting Started

1. Open Volumizer — it appears in your menu bar.

2. Grant Screen & System Audio Recording permission when prompted (System Settings → Privacy & Security).

3. Running apps appear automatically. Adjust volume, mute, or route audio per app.

Frequently Asked Questions

Why don't I see any apps?

Make sure Screen & System Audio Recording permission is granted in System Settings → Privacy & Security. Restart Volumizer after granting.

Why doesn't Safari volume control work?

Safari needs to be actively playing audio for its volume to be controllable. Start playing media in Safari, then adjust its volume in Volumizer.

How does the subscription work?

Volumizer offers a 3-day free trial. After the trial, it's $0.99/month to continue using all features. You can cancel anytime in System Settings → Apple ID → Media & Purchases → Subscriptions → Volumizer.

What's included?

All features: per-app volume control and mute, 10-band EQ, output routing, output groups (multi-device), Focus Audio, and custom keyboard shortcuts.
